Chi Alpha Campus Ministries:

Chi Alpha (XA) is a Christian Fellowship on campus that I am heavily involved in. WVU XA has grown so much in its short time on campus. With about 20 people involved three years ago, XA now has about 120 regulars attending. The top picture is of all the leaders for the upcoming year, picture about 6 people per person and you can start to see how many people are involved as a whole. I joined my freshman year and I became a small group leader my sophomore year. I lead a bible study of about 5 guys (bottom picture) every week and I attend all of the retreats and events. I can honestly say that my friendships through XA are the strongest of any in my life. XA has taught me so much about leadership, friendship, organizing group functions, and the balance of social and work life.
